Cirque du Soleil

My role as CRM Strategist for Cirque du Soleil's North American branch and Blue Man Group was truly a once-in-a-lifetime opportunity.

My responsibilities ranged from designing and developing email marketing campaigns for over 6 million people worldwide, building automated customer journeys, and managing CDS + BMG's customer databases.

Ziff Davis

Ziff Davis is where I learned the ropes of digital marketing, e-commerce, and data manipulation.

I was fortunate enough to work on many well-known brands under the ZD banner including AskMen, IGN, Mashable.

It was at ZD that I discovered my passion for web design and copywriting.

Wienstein & Gavino's

W&G has been a staple of Montreal's world-famous restaurant scene for generations, and has remained so due to a tactical investment in their online presence.

As a Montreal-native and lover of Italian food, it was an honour to design assets for their new range of store-bought products, as well as their social media accounts.
